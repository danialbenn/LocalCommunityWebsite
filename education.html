HISTORY


The township of Bandar Baru Bangi, which includes Seksyen 9, was built in the end of the 1980s and start of the 1990s because of to the growing urbanisation and population in the Malaysian region around Kuala Lumpur. There would be a mix of homes, businesses, & manufacturing sectors in this self-sufficient village. The development was planned to offer up-to-date homes and amenities, and it was placed in a convenient location close to main thoroughfares.
